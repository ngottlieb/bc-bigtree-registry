Welcome to the BC BigTree Registry!

The BC BigTree Registry is maintained by the Faculty of Forestry at the University of British Columbia. It's an evolving project whose mandate is "To identify, describe, monitor, and conserve the largest trees of each species within British Columbia, and to educate and enlist the help of its citizens in this task."

To view the registry in table view, visit its home on the UBC Forestry website. You can also find more information about how to contribute to the database there.

Please note!
  • If you visit these trees, respect private property and view from a distance.
  • Exercise extreme caution> when travelling on logging roads.
  • File a trip plan when travelling in remote areas.
  • View trees from afar to avoid damaging tree roots and vegetation.
